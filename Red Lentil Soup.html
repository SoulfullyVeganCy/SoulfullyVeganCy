Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients
1 tb olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
1 large potato
2 large carrots
1 red bell pepper
1 medium ripe tomato, skin removed
300 g red lentils, rinsed and drained
1 bay leaf
1 litre hot water
1 vegan vegetable stock cube crumbled,
Salt and pepper to taste



Instructions:
1. Cut all the veggies evenly.
2. Heat oil in a large pot.
3. Add the onion and saut√© until translucent.
4. Add the veggies and cook until soft but not mushy.
5. Add the rest of the ingredients, stir, and bring to a boil.
6. Reduce heat, cover, and cook until the lentils are soft.
7. Puree using an immersion blender, food processor, or regular blender until it reaches a smooth consistency. You could also leave the soup chunky.
8. Adjust seasoning and serve.
