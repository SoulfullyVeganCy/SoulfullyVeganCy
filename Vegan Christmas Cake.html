Vegan Christmas Cake

Ingredients:
120 gr olive oil
170 gr brandy
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tb allspice
2 tsp instant coffee powder
200 gr maple syrup/agave/carob syrup or a mixture of them
400 gr flour
4 tsp BP
Pinch of salt
240 gr chopped walnuts/pecans
100 gr chopped dates

For each feeding: 25 ml coffee liquor + 25 ml brandy stirred together.
For decorating: Marzipan and Icing.



Instructions:
1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
2. Mix all the wet ingredients in another bowl.
3. Add the wet mixture to the dry and mix well to combine. (The mixture will be quite thick)
4. Bake in a 21 cm pan at 180° C for about 40 minutes.
5. The next day feed the cake, and again every other day, for 2-3 times.
6. A Day after the last feeding, top the cake with marzipan, then icing and decorate as you like.
